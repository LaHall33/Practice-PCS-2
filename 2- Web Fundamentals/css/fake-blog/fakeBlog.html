Welcome!! Today we place the spotlight on DESIREE!

Desiree, is a profilic author celebrated for her gritty yet heartfelt narratives. Yes, that's that author known for her crazy family stories! She has made her mark in the contemporary fiction genre with her stories centering love, loss, and personal growth while being comedic. Her readers eagerly anticipate her next literary offering. Check out her personal blog for a list of great reads and to become a member of her community. Feel free to read a story and let us know what you think in the comments!
